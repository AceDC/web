Our Carrier

The EV industry is an exciting, multi-disciplinary area bringing together passionate professionals who want to make the world a better place through innovative electric mobility applications

No other industry offers the opportunity to harness purpose, problem-solving ability and pioneering leadership as the nascent EV industry in India today. Now is the time for innovative, bold ideas to come to the fore and swing India’s EV potential to the next orbit.

As a specialist company in the electric vehicle powertrain market, Electra EV is continuously innovating to bring new solutions to the forefront in its core areas of powertrain optimization and energy utilization.

Electra EV is a meeting place for some of the finest engineering minds dedicated to the creation of electric vehicles. As of the financial year ending 31 March 2020, twenty-five per cent of the total electric cars in India ran with an Electra EV powertrain inside them. This is a testament to the capability of the motivated team of professionals at Electra EV. They innovate every day to create powertrain solutions for the world’s most challenging markets.
